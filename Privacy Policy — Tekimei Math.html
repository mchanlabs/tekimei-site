Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 1, 2026

Tekimei Math ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of all users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how our iOS application Tekimei Math handles information.

Information We Do Not Collect

Tekimei Math is designed with privacy first. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personally identifiable information. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use analytics services that track individual users.
  • We do not share any data with third parties.
  • We do not display advertising of any kind.

Data Stored on Your Device

All app data — including player name, scores, progress, streaks, and mastery records — is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage APIs. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

If you delete the app, all locally stored data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

Tekimei Math is designed for use by children under parental supervision. Because we collect no personal information whatsoever, the app is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Apple's guidelines for apps in the Kids category.

We encourage parents and guardians to supervise their children's use of all apps, including ours.

In-App Purchases

Tekimei Math offers optional in-app purchases processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not handle, store, or have access to any payment information. Please refer to Apple's Privacy Policy for information on how purchase data is handled.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
