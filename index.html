The Curriculum

9 groups. Counting
to calculus-ready.

The Tekimei Curriculum is a structured progression built on how kids actually learn math — step by step, skill by skill.

G1

Early Counting

Numbers, sequences, and foundational number sense.

G2

Early Addition

Single-digit facts and the building blocks of arithmetic.

G3

Addition & Subtraction

Fluency with two-digit operations and mental math.

G4

Multiplication & Division

Times tables, fact families, and inverse relationships.

G5

Fractions

Parts of a whole, equivalent fractions, and operations.

G6

Algebra Foundations

Variables, expressions, and early equation solving.

G7

Ratios & Proportions

Scaling, percentages, and proportional reasoning.

G8

Linear Equations

Slope, graphing, and solving systems of equations.

G9

Advanced Algebra

Quadratics, functions, and pre-calculus readiness.

About Tekimei

Built by a parent,
for young learners.

Tekimei (適明) means something like "fitting clarity" — the idea that the right knowledge, learned the right way, creates a kind of brightness in a child's mind.

We built Tekimei Math because we believe kids deserve more than random flashcard apps. They deserve a structured, thoughtful curriculum that meets them where they are and grows with them.

Math is just the beginning. Tekimei is designed from the ground up to eventually support Reading, Science, and beyond — all with the same structured, mastery-based approach.
